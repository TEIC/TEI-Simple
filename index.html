Tei Simple

TEI Simple aims to define a new highly-constrained and prescriptive subset of the Text Encoding Initiative (TEI) Guidelines suited to the representation of early modern and modern books, a processing model which permits modern web applications to easily present the encoded texts, and processes to describe the encoding status and richness of a TEI digital text.
